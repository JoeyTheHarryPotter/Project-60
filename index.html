My Favorite Hobby
Reading Books



My favorite hobby is reading intresting and fun books, for example the Percy Jackson series. It's fun and entertaining. Also, books don't make you bored and you have tons off options! You can gain a lot of knowledge from them. It can also help you study for a school test. If you read high grade level books, you can learn a lot of vocabulary words along the way and you'll be ahead of the class! You can also imagine the plot and events of the story coming along in your head. This is why reading books is so important to me.
